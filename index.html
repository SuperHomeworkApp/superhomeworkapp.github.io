DESIGN

It's the solution you wish you've always had.


SuperHomework does it for you.

Gone are the days of announcements and agenda books, and the web is here to stay. Your teacher has probably already adopted this change, and posts their homework on a website or other electronic medium. But there's one current problem with this system that makes it undesirable compared to a traditional agenda book: there is no organization, and you as a student end up wasting time navigating across multiple sites and sources while trying to do your homework.

SuperHomework can simplify all of this. It automatically pulls the nightly homework from your teachers' websites and puts them into a clear and accessible format. Simply open the app to view — that's it! Or with SuperHomework Pro, get notifications with a summary of your work every afternoon without lifting a finger.

Quick and Convenient

All your assignments, all right there. SuperHomework quickly and efficiently downloads the latest assignments from your teachers’ sites. If your school isn’t registered with SuperHomework yet, you can still add your own assignments and customize their description, due date, icon, and more.

Notifications

Wouldn’t it be awesome if your agenda book could remind you about what you put inside it? With SuperHomework Pro, it can do that too. Set your own reminders for all your assignments, or get a daily summary at the time of your choosing.

Works with Google Classroom™

If your teacher uses Google Classroom™ instead of a traditional website, SuperHomework has you covered. Simply sign in with your Google account and the latest assignments will appear right alongside everything else.

FOR TEACHERS

You don't have to do a thing.

If you have a blog, you're all set.

Seriously. If your site is compatible with SuperHomework — and chances are it will be — then you don't have to configure anything. SuperHomework will automatically pull information from your site for students to recieve, all without any extra menus or submissions on your part.


Compatibility requires that your school is available within SuperHomework and that your website is set up in a blog format with RSS enabled.
