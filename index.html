How does it work?

Does it for you

Never write in an agenda book again — just open the SuperHomework app to see your agenda at a glance, or get notifications for instant reminders.

Get started

Add your classes

If you're a student or parent, choose them in the app. If you're a teacher or school administrator, it's easy to make your classes visible to students.

SuperHomework currently works with a number of teacher blogging websites and Google Classroom classes, and more options will be added soon.
