SuperHomework

SuperHomework automatically organizes your homework, right from your phone.

What is SuperHomework?

Notifications

Know when your teachers post new assignments, and get reminders for upcoming due dates.

Agenda

All your homework in one place. Tap on an assignment to view its content or open the original link.

Integrations

Everything in SuperHomework is automatic and works with Google Classroom, blogs and more.

Everything is automatic.

SuperHomework connects to your favorite integrations and automatically listens for new assignments. When your teacher posts tonight’s homework, you’ll get a notification and the assignment will appear instantly in the app.

More integrations are on the way, and you can always add your own assignments manually, with up to two reminder notifications at the dates you choose. SuperHomework can also send you a daily summary of everything in your Agenda.

SuperHomework is available on the App Store for free.
